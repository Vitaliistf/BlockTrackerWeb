FAQ

  • How to register?

    In order, to register an account you need to go to register page and fill in all the fields and follow instructions. You will need to confirm the registration through a letter that will come to your e-mail, and then you will be able to log in and start using our service.

    OR

    You may just sign up using Google or Binance account by clicking on the icons below.

  • How to change password?

    If you need to change password, you can go to the login page and click Forgot password? fill in your e-mail and new password, then confirm that the account is really yours, and you will be able to log in using new password.

  • Which coins are supported?

    Our service is working with KuCoin API, so we support all the coins that are supported on KuCoin.
